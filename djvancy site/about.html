About DJ Vancy

DJ Vancy, born David Gitonga, is a Kenyan DJ known for delivering refined, high-impact performances built on precision and musical discipline.

With over a decade behind the decks, his journey spans intimate spaces to high-energy club settings, developing a deep understanding of timing, transitions, and crowd psychology.

As a resident DJ at SilverLuna Gardens, he commands peak-hour sets with confidence, blending Afrobeat, Amapiano, Reggae, Dancehall, and Kenyan sounds into a cohesive experience.
